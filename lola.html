All About Lola (Mum)

Lola is a super sweet little girl, she is very smart but reserved, she is very good at reading a room! She is extremely tolerant of children, in her eyes little kids could do no wrong,

She is a natural born mother! She dotes on her babies, giving them all the love and milk they desire, she really sets the up for success. Lola came from a show line pedigree, and she can work it!

Her natural show ring gait is the biggest give away! She truly is a little princess when out walking, she turns heads! That said, she too is a natural born hunter!
