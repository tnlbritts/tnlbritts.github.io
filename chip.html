All About Chip (the child)

Chippy is a sweet soul, just like his dad, he aims to please and is very affectionate, he too is a superstar at any activity he is thrown into, his biggest love is scent work, but is great at Hoopers, tricks and everyday obedience. He is praise and food driven.

His natural bobtail never stops wiggling, it is so cute, he is just the happiest dog you would ever meet!

Chippy is the biggest of the 2020 litter, he is a Lean, muscled and fit man, weighing in at 17kg and a height of 55cm (floor to shoulders).

He has both the traditional French Brittany looks with the American Brittany build.
