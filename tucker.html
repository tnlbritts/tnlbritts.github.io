All About Tucker (Dad)

Tucker is a personality plus little guy, he aims to please and is very affectionate, he is a superstar at any activity he is thrown into, his biggest loves are Agility & Scent work, but is great at tricks and everyday obedience - he is praise driven.
He has always been the star in all his classes! One of his litter mates was an acting dog, must be in the genes! He is a natural bobtail; which in his first litter, he passed on to all his offspring
Tucker is a lean, muscled and fit man, weighing in at 15kg and a height of 50cm (floor to shoulders), he has traditional the French Brittany looks. He comes from hunting stock with built in skills!
Although never trained in the hunt he would have made a great hunting companion, he has exceptional skills in the bush and water
He is happiest at home with his people being a smooch, he does love running free in big open spaces though!
